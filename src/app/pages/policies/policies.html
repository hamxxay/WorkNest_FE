Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025

We strive to provide a seamless experience. If you are not satisfied, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the booking start time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made 24–48 hours before the booking start time are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the booking start time are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

Contact our support team at sales@worknestpk.com with your booking ID and reason for cancellation. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Bookings cancelled outside the eligible window.
  • Bookings where the space was used even partially.
  • Promotional or discounted bookings marked as non-refundable.

Contact

For refund queries, email sales@worknestpk.com or call +92 308 0256000.

Booking Policy

Last updated: June 2025

These guidelines govern how bookings are made, managed, and honoured at WorkNest.

Making a Booking

  • Bookings can be made online through the WorkNest platform by registered users.
  • A booking is confirmed only after successful payment processing.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details and assigned space.

Space Assignment

  • WorkNest uses an auto-assignment system to allocate the best available space of the requested type.
  • The assigned space (room number / code) will be shared in your booking confirmation.
  • WorkNest reserves the right to reassign spaces in exceptional circumstances while ensuring equivalent or better alternatives.

Usage Rules

  • Spaces must be used only during the booked time window.
  • Subletting or transferring a booking to another party is not permitted.
  • Damage to the space or its equipment will be charged to the booking holder.
  • Smoking, illegal activities, or disruptive behaviour will result in immediate termination of the booking without refund.

Modifications

Booking modifications (date/time changes) are subject to availability and must be requested at least 24 hours in advance by contacting sales@worknestpk.com.

Contact
